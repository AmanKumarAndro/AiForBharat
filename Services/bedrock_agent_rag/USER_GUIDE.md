# 📱 Farmer Voice AI - User Guide

> Complete guide for farmers and users to interact with the Voice AI system

## 🌟 Welcome

Farmer Voice AI is your personal farming assistant that understands and speaks Hindi. Ask any farming question using your voice or by typing, and get instant answers with helpful videos and resources.

## 🎯 What Can You Ask?

### 1. Farming Advice
Ask about crops, planting, harvesting, fertilizers, and farming techniques.

**Examples:**
- "गेहूं की बुवाई कब करें?" (When to sow wheat?)
- "धान की फसल में खाद कितनी डालें?" (How much fertilizer for rice crop?)
- "टमाटर में कीड़े लगे हैं, क्या करें?" (Tomatoes have pests, what to do?)
- "मिट्टी की जांच कैसे करें?" (How to test soil?)

### 2. Video Tutorials
Ask for videos to learn farming techniques visually.

**Examples:**
- "टमाटर की खेती का वीडियो दिखाओ" (Show tomato farming video)
- "ड्रिप सिंचाई कैसे करें वीडियो" (How to do drip irrigation video)
- "गेहूं की कटाई का ट्यूटोरियल" (Wheat harvesting tutorial)
- "जैविक खेती सीखना है" (Want to learn organic farming)

### 3. Government Schemes
Ask about PM-KISAN, subsidies, and government programs.

**Examples:**
- "PM-KISAN की अगली किस्त कब आएगी?" (When is next PM-KISAN installment?)
- "सोलर पंप पर सब्सिडी कैसे मिलेगी?" (How to get solar pump subsidy?)
- "किसान क्रेडिट कार्ड कैसे बनाएं?" (How to make Kisan Credit Card?)
- "फसल बीमा योजना क्या है?" (What is crop insurance scheme?)

### 4. Market Prices
Ask about current crop prices and mandi rates.

**Examples:**
- "आज मंडी में गेहूं की कीमत?" (Today's wheat price in mandi?)
- "प्याज का भाव क्या चल रहा है?" (What is onion rate?)
- "धान का MSP कितना है?" (What is paddy MSP?)

## 📱 How to Use

### Using Voice (Recommended)

1. **Tap the Microphone Button** 🎤
   - The button will turn red when recording
   - You'll see a waveform animation

2. **Speak Your Question in Hindi**
   - Speak clearly and naturally
   - No need to shout or speak slowly
   - Ask complete questions

3. **Wait for Processing**
   - You'll see: "सुन रहे हैं" (Listening)
   - Then: "सोच रहे हैं" (Thinking)
   - Finally: "जवाब दे रहे हैं" (Answering)

4. **Listen to the Answer**
   - Audio will play automatically
   - You can also read the Hindi text on screen
   - Videos and links will appear below

### Using Text

1. **Type Your Question**
   - Use the text input box
   - Type in Hindi or Hinglish
   - Press Send button

2. **Read the Answer**
   - Answer appears in Hindi text
   - Videos and links shown below
   - Tap speaker icon to hear the answer

## 🎬 Understanding Responses

### Text Answer
The main answer to your question in simple Hindi.

**Example:**
```
गेहूं की बुवाई नवंबर के पहले हफ्ते में करें। 
मिट्टी का तापमान 20-25 डिग्री होना चाहिए।
बीज की दूरी 20 सेमी रखें।
```

### Video Recommendations 📹
When you ask for videos or tutorials, you'll see:

```
📹 टमाटर की खेती - संपूर्ण गाइड
   Channel: Krishi Gyan
   [Watch Video →]
```

Tap "Watch Video" to open YouTube.

### Web Links 🌐
For government schemes and prices, you'll see:

```
🌐 PM-KISAN Official Portal
   Check your payment status and register
   [Visit Website →]
```

Tap "Visit Website" to open the link.

### Source Information
At the bottom, you'll see where the answer came from:

```
📚 Source: ICAR Wheat Manual, 2023
⏱️ Response Time: 3.2 seconds
```

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

### Speaking Tips
- ✅ Speak naturally in Hindi
- ✅ Ask complete questions
- ✅ Use common farming terms
- ❌ Don't speak too fast
- ❌ Avoid background noise

### Question Tips
- ✅ Be specific: "गेहूं में पीला रतुआ रोग" instead of "गेहूं में बीमारी"
- ✅ Include crop name: "धान की खेती" instead of just "खेती"
- ✅ Ask one thing at a time
- ❌ Don't ask multiple questions together

### Follow-up Questions
The system remembers your conversation! You can ask follow-up questions:

**First Question:**
```
You: "गेहूं की बुवाई कब करें?"
AI: "नवंबर के पहले हफ्ते में..."
```

**Follow-up:**
```
You: "और खाद कितनी डालें?"
AI: "गेहूं के लिए 120 किलो यूरिया..."
```

The AI remembers you're asking about wheat!

All conversation data is automatically deleted after 7 days.

## ❓ Troubleshooting

### "Could not understand audio"
**Problem:** Voice not recognized  
**Solution:**
- Speak louder and clearer
- Reduce background noise
- Try typing the question instead

### "No response received"
**Problem:** Network timeout  
**Solution:**
- Check internet connection
- Try again in a few seconds
- Use text input if voice fails

### "Answer not relevant"
**Problem:** Wrong or unclear answer  
**Solution:**
- Rephrase your question more specifically
- Include crop name and specific issue
- Try asking in simpler words

### Videos not loading
**Problem:** YouTube links not opening  
**Solution:**
- Check internet connection
- Update YouTube app
- Try opening link in browser
